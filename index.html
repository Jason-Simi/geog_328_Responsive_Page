Portofino

Portofino, Italy, is a beautiful city nestled along the Italian Riviera and is well known as Coastal Paradise that enchants visitors by its breathtaking natural beauty and its spirited history. Its historical significance stems from its time as a traditional marine village dating back to Roman times when it was known as Portus Delphini where it was a major port for the many civilizations that owned the land.
