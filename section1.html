Portofino is a port city in the region of Liguria that is now known for its beautiful landscape and buildings. Portofino, however, has deep historical roots dating back to Roman times where it served as a port and a fishing village. During these times, the houses began to be painted in bright colors for sailors to recognize the port that they were looking to dock at. Due to the colorful nature of the city and the environment, Portofino was later determined to be an admirable place to live and has been a monument of leisure to those on the coast.
